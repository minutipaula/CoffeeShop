Born out of Melbourne’s hyper-competitive neighborhood cafes and cross-pollinated with Copenhagen's clean architectural philosophy, coffeshop strips away the noise surrounding specialty coffee.

"We do not dark roast to mask defects; we source flawlessly so the roast can remain translucent."

Through direct partnerships with micro-lot farms spanning Ethiopia, Colombia, and Brazil, we acquire coffees that express striking historical identities. Hand-roasted in small 12kg batches using tailored custom profiling curves, we offer unparalleled clarity across both filter and espresso Extractions.
